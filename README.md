# Global Electric Vehicle Sales Dashboard 🚗⚡

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes global electric vehicle (EV) sales trends across different regions and vehicle models. The dashboard provides insights into the growth of the EV market by visualizing key metrics such as total sales, revenue distribution, BEV adoption rate, and units sold across regions.

The goal of this project is to transform raw EV sales data into meaningful insights using **data visualization and business intelligence techniques**.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Global EV sales show **steady growth from 2018 to 2025**.
* **China and Europe dominate the EV market** in terms of total sales.
* **BEV adoption is increasing globally**, indicating a strong transition toward fully electric vehicles.
* Model-level analysis highlights the **best-performing EV models** across regions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes the following interactive visualizations:

* **Total Sales KPI** – Overview of total EV units sold
* **Revenue Analysis** – Total revenue generated from EV sales
* **BEV Share** – Average BEV adoption across years
* **Sales Trend Analysis** – Year-wise EV sales growth
* **Regional Comparison** – EV sales distribution by region
* **Model Performance** – Units sold by EV model
* **Interactive Filters** – Region and model filters for dynamic exploration
